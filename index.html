Features

Personalized Daily Planner
  • ✅ Calendar View – View all tasks by date in a clean calendar layout.

  • ✅ Time Slots – Assign specific times to each task (time blocking).

  • ✅ Drag & Drop Tasks – Easily reorder tasks in the planner.
Smart Task Management
  • ✅ Auto-Suggestions – Get task suggestions based on your habits or previous entries.

  • ✅ Recurring Tasks – Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, or on custom intervals.

  • ✅ Priority Levels – Label tasks as High, Medium, or Low importance.
Productivity Boosters
  • ✅ Reminders & Notifications – Alerts before deadlines or at preferred times.

  • ✅ Progress Tracking – Show % of completed tasks or progress bars.

  • ✅ Streaks & Motivation – Visual streaks or motivational quotes for daily completions.
